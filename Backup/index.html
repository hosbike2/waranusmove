What's WaranusMove?

A Machine Learning

to analysis structure of abstract for any article An abstract is simply a summary of an article in one paragraph and can use as an overview. Generally, abstracts are limited to 200 to 300 words, but the exact word limit will be stated by the publication, conference, or organization requesting the abstract. Reader often read abstracts at first ,they will likely get the key result and conclusions. Each major of scientific papers and include writing style of academic have a different format. A well written abstract will make the reader want to learn more about research, read paper, or attend in presentation.

The format of abstract

will depend on the work being abstracted. An abstract of a scientific or any research paper will contain elements not found in an abstract of a literature article. The components of an abstract are Background, Purpose, Method, Result and Discussion.

Background : Why do you care about the problem? This section should include the importance of your work and the impact it might have if successful.

Purpose : What is the objective of the research? The purpose section of an informative abstract should state the primary objectives of the experiment or research.

Method : How did you solving on the problem? In this section you explain clearly how you carried out your study, how the data were analyzed and how the experimental or sampling design.

Result : What was after the method was applied? Result section is to objectively present your key results, without interpretation. Summaries of paper may appear either in the text or Figures.

Discussion : What are the practical applications from findings or implications of research? What would be the next step in your study? The function of the Discussion is to interpret your results in light of what was already known, to explain our new understanding of the problem after taking your results.
